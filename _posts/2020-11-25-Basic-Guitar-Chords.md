## Basic Chords
Most pop songs are made of the same chord patterns.  Know these four chords and you'll be well on your way to knowing guitar!

### A
2ND fret D G B

### C 
3rd fret: A 2nd D, 1st: B
### D
3rd fret: B , 2nd G,E
### E
2nd fret: A, D, 1st: G
### G
3rd fret: A, E 2nd: D
